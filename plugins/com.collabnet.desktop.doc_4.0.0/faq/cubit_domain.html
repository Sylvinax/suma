What is the Lab Management Domain?

The Lab Management Domain is the top-level organizational unit in Lab Management.

All other resources in Lab Management - users, hosts, profiles, and projects - belong to the Lab Management Domain. If a host is not assigned to any particular project, it is said that the host belongs to the Domain.
