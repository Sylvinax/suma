Lab Management concepts

  • What does the Lab Management integration provide?
    When you add a Lab Management site, you can do a number of things from your Desktop, without having to switch to a different application.
  • What is the Lab Management Domain?
    The Lab Management Domain is the top-level organizational unit in Lab Management.
  • What is the Lab Management Manager?
    The Lab Management Manager is the main node that Lab Management uses to run itself.
  • What is a Client Node?
    Client Nodes refer to virtual or physical servers.
  • What is a Lab Management host?
    Hosts refer to nodes that Lab Management uses to run itself, such as the Lab Management Manager Node, and Client Nodes.
  • What are Lab Management user roles?
    There are four roles in Lab Management from which to choose that you can assign to users.
  • What is a Lab Management project?
    A Lab Management Project is a logical grouping of resources in Lab Management that include users, roles, hosts and profiles.
  • What is a Lab Management profile?
    A Lab Management Profile is a machine and human-readable definition that can be used to construct a running operating system environment on a computer.
  • How does tunneling work?
    Lab Management makes setting up and managing SSH tunnels faster and easier than having to do it manually.
