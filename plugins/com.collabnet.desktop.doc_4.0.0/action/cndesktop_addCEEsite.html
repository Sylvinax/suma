Add a new site

When you add a CollabNet Enterprise Edition site, you can access your projects and project tools depending on your role.

  1. In the CollabNet Sites view, click the Add CollabNet Site icon

    .
  2. In the Add a CollabNet Site dialog, select the type CollabNet Enterprise Edition and click Next.
  3. Specify the domain URL for the site. For example, https://www.cee.mycompany.net/.
  4. Enter a descriptive name for the site and click Next.
  5. Provide your username and password to access the site.
  6. Click Finish.
Your CollabNet Enterprise Edition site appears in the CollabNet Sites view. You can expand it to access your projects' source code repositories and tracking tools.
