Plan and monitor your project

The dynamic planning features of TeamForge help you effectively utilize Agile processes in your project.

  • Define the scope of your project
    Defining scope is an iterative, interactive process for the product owner. As you go through it, you'll find elements of your scope expanding, shrinking or changing shape in response to feedback from analyzing and planning out the work.
  • Organize your project with planning folders
    Create and populate all the planning folders you need to capture the work you are planning.
  • Manage your sprint with the planning board
    The planning board is the hub for your TeamForge project's Agile planning activities. It enables you to plan and monitor the features that are required in each sprint (or iteration) in order to release your product. In the planning board, tracker artifacts are represented as cards and displayed in ranked sequence. You can drag and drop cards to assign them to planning folders or change their rank.
  • Manage tasks with the Agile task board
    The CollabNet Desktop supports an Agile task board for TeamForge.
  • Monitor project output
    As a product owner, you can regularly test and measure the features your team produces, involving real users as much as possible.
  • Monitor project progress
    Use a variety of charts to help you monitor the progress of your project.
  • Dynamic planning in TeamForge - FAQ
    TeamForge helps you maximize your team's effectiveness by keeping you in close touch with the multiple moving targets facing your project.
