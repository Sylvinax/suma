Add a pre-configured CollabNet community site

The CollabNet Sites view provides some pre-configured CollabNet community sites where you can keep up with product updates, latest downloads and more.

The openCollabNet, CloudForge (cloudforge.com) and CollabNet TeamForge Community sites are included in the CollabNet Sites view. If you have already registered at any of these sites, you can connect to it by supplying your credentials. If you have not registered, you can set up a new account.

Follow these steps if you discarded one of these sites, and want to add it again:

  1. In the CollabNet Sites view, click the Add CollabNet Site icon.
  2. In the Add a CollabNet Site dialog, select a CollabNet site under "Add a pre-configured site" and click Next. Site details such as type and description are populated.
  3. Make your edits (optional) and click Next. The text you provide for the description will be displayed as the site's name in the CollabNet Sites view.
  4. Enter your account details.
    • If you have already registered at the site, supply your credentials.
    • To register, click Create new account and enter the required information.
  5. Click Finish.
The site appears in the CollabNet Sites view. Expand it to explore the site.
