Manage tunnels

In the Manage tunnels screen, you can add or remove tunnels, and open or close ones that you've created.

The Manage tunnels screen displays the details for all your tunnels. The Usage field is populated by the system depending on your Lab Management role.
  1. In the CollabNet Sites view where you have added a Lab Management site, right-click, and select Manage tunnels.
  2. In the Manage Tunnels dialog, do any of the following.
    • To add a new tunnel, click Create Tunnel.
    • To delete a tunnel, right-click, and select Remove tunnel.
    • To open a tunnel that you've configured, right-click, and select Open tunnel.
    • To close a tunnel that is open, right-click, and select Close tunnel.
