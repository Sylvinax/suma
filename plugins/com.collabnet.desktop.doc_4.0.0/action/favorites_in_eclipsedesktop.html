Customize the CollabNet Sites view

CollabNet Desktop favorites are TeamForge projects and tools that you use frequently. You can customize your CollabNet Sites view by selecting the projects and tools you want to see while browsing the site.

  • Work with your favorite projects
    By specifying your favorite projects, you can have only those projects displayed in the CollabNet Sites tree.
  • Work with your favorite tools
    You can choose your favorite TeamForge tools at the site level, project level or across all projects and sites.
